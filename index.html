Attention Mode

This mode flashes the screen and (if supported) the torch in a rapid, unpredictable pattern while reading captured text aloud. It may not be safe for people sensitive to flashing lights.

CEO Mode

You speak the “You:” lines. The app waits based on your speaking speed, then speaks the “Employee:” lines.
